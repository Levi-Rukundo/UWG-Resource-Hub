Academic Support

UWG offers several academic resources to help students succeed in their courses, develop stronger study habits, and stay on track for graduation.

Tutoring Services

Tutoring programs help students understand course material and improve performance in subjects such as math, science, and writing.

Writing Center

The Writing Center helps students improve essays, research papers, and other writing assignments through one-on-one assistance.

Academic Advising

Academic advisors guide students in choosing courses, understanding degree requirements, and planning their academic paths.

Study Skills Support

Students can receive help with note-taking, time management, test preparation, and effective study strategies.
