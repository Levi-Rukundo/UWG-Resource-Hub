About This Website

The UWG Campus Resource Hub is a student-created project designed to organize campus support resources in one simple and easy-to-use place.

What is the purpose of this website?

This website was created to help students quickly locate important campus resources in one organized and easy-to-use place.

Who is this website for?

The website is designed for college students, especially first-year, transfer, and international students who may need help finding support.

Is this an official UWG website?

No. This is a student-created project for an academic course and is not an official University of West Georgia website.

What could be added in the future?

Future improvements could include direct links to offices, expanded contact information, search tools, and more detailed campus resource listings.
